RULES
These are the rules of TSW.  For other matters, use common sense.  


* Limited player stealing or player killing.  


* Your presence here is a privilege not a right.  Obey any orders from gods,

and you will be fine.  Deletion does not require a reason, appeals are to be

sent to the implementors.  


* Sitting around idle with a client is hazardous to your health.  We don't

recommend it, but keeping your characer online by using triggers is not

against the rules.  


* Botting (Ex: using a client to level your characters) while you are away

from the keys is not acceptable.  A few triggers to assist with levelling

will be tolerated, but if you are found to be letting a bot run, you may

lose your character, levels, or any number of things.  


* Multiple logins are allowed with restrictions.  Read help multiplaying for

details.  


* No kill stealing.  This means you cannot attack a mob someone else is

fighting, unless they ask you to.  Also avoid attacking fleeing mobs.  


* Cheating will not be tolerated.  This includes accepting favors from gods

and exploiting bugs.  If you find a bug, report it, then do not use it.  


* Harrasment of any form will not be tolerated.  This includes sending

people tells after they've asked you to stop, as well as repeatedly

complaining about someone to the Administration when they are leaving you

alone.  


* Discussing who plays what characters on the game over public channels is

strictly prohibited unless the player in question has given you explicit

permission to do so.  Many people like a little bit of privacy, the admin

staff often prefer to not have to worry about people treating their

characters differently because they also admin the game.  The first offense

you may get off with as light as a warning, (or perhaps a warning balefire),

the second time, you lose all of your equipment (even the quest equipment),

the third time, more drastic measures will be taken.  (Public channels

consist of all channels that you can toggle on or off, including minion)


* Changes to these rules are at the discretion of the immortals, and may be

done without warning, notice, or even evidence of sanity.  Walk softly, and

carry a big stick.
