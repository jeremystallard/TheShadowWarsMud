gleader_commands
join  - allows gleaders to join new members to the guild

accept        - allows players to join the guild once offered

refuse        - allows players to refuse to join a guild once it has been offered

gtitle  - allows gleader to set a guild title

gmute  - toggles mute on a guildmember

ginvis        - allows gleaders to go invis to rest of guild

gmembers      - shows a list of all members in the guild.  Caution: this can lag the MUD
