SKILLS WEAVES SKILL WEAVE
The skills and weaves commands are used to display your character's list

of available skills (or weaves, as the case may be).  They are listed in

order of level with their trained value.

 

The flags listed with each skill or weave have the following meaning:

(t) - Teachable skill/weave, no restriction.

(f) - Female only skill/weave.

(m) - Male only skill/weave.

(n) - Not teachable skill/weave.

(g) - Granted skill/weave.

 

!   - Means the skill/weave is talent only.
