GATE
Syntax: cast gate  


The gate weave is a far simpler, far more direct, far quicker means of

transport.  The gate weave open a doorway from one physical point to another

by using flows of Air, Earth, and Spirit.  


Men do this by boring a hole in the Pattern, so to speak, while women do

this by making the Pattern in both locations identical (LoC, 37, When Battle

Begins, p491).  Moghedien and Rand are of the opinion that using the wrong

method would be catastrophically bad.  


Stepping through the gate, one instantly changes location.  


To gain enough knowledge from a place to be able to open a gateway to or

from it, require the use of the 'study' command.  


After studying a room for 5 ticks, the secret room key will be revealed.  


See also 'help traveling' for more information.  See also 'help seal' for

how to seal a open gateway.
