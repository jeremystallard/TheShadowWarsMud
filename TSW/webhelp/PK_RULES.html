PK_RULES
- A PK fight will only be valid if the players are within 7 levels range of

each other.  For example a level 14 PK will only get a valid kill if he

attacks and kills someone in the level range 7-21. 

 

- If a PK is grouped with a non-PK and wins a fight they won't get a valid

kill out of the fight.

 

- Attacking a non-PK will not count as a valid PK kill and will not generate

a corpse to be looted. 

 

- In a valid PK fight the winner has the right to loot the other player's

corpse at the end of the fight.  

 

- In a PK fight the player that lost the fight will lose an amount of

experience based on their level, that amount of experience will be

automatically given to the one who killed the player.  If the player in

question doesn't have any experience they will have to get extra experience

to reach next level.

 

- Engaging in a PK fight while a player is RPing is not acceptable and not

allowed be the person a PK or not. 

 

- PK, Quest Equipment and Tokens can't be looted. 

 

- Non-PK are not allowed to loot a PK's corpse.  If you are not a PK and you

find a PK's corpse on the ground leave it be.  Getting any items from such

corpses will result on the non-PK being forced to become a PK by the

Immortals. 


- PK people can give looted items to non-PK people but they must give it

instead of the non-PK person taking it from the corpse.  Taking anything

from a corpse that is not yours will be considered looting.

 

- Once a player is PKed they will be given a timer of 30 minutes in which

they can look for their corpse or gather equipment without being attacked by

another PK.  The timer will reset if the player quits and then logs on again

before the 30 minutes are up.

 

- If any players are found to be abusing the PK system to get their ranks

higher and get the monthly prizes they will have their PK rank reset or set

to negative up to the Immortal that catches them.

 

Remember that the rules are set in place so everyone can have a good

experience and have fun.
