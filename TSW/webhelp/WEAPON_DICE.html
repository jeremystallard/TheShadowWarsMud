WEAPON_DICE
These are the values the server generates automatically when

converting a merc-format mobile to ROM format.  A higher

number of dice gives less variance in damage.  There is no

dice-bonus on weapon dice.


      level:   dice 

        1:     1d8

        2:     2d5

        3:     2d5

        5:     2d6

       10:     4d5

       20:     5d5

       30:     5d7

       50:     5d11
