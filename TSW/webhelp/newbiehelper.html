Welcome to the ShadowWars.

 

To become a Newbie Helper please read the requirements first.

1. You must be over level 30

2. You must have played with us activily for atleast 6 months.

3. Be familiar with mobs and eq that are below 30, so you can help the newer players.

4. Understand the rules of the game.

 

If you can do all of those please send a note to admin in the immortal area for concideration to be a newbiehelper.

 

 

Once you are a Newbie Helper there are a few things to follow.

1. Do not validate names that are book names, or names that do not fit into the WoT world.

2. Never validate your own alts.

3. Never validate a player unless they have an approprate appearance.

 

Using the command review (player) will help you to see what you need before you validate them.


Using the command setapp   will let you

change an unvalidated players appearance for them.

 

For names, the restrictions of book names that do not fit in

a wheel of time world are names like mat, Lan, Rand, Elayne, Batman,

Skywalker, Vader, Scooby, and others of this sort.

 

Names that are acceptable are any found in the Wheel of Time name

generator at www.wotmud.org/names

and most other names found in Real Life situations, although RJ has a 

habit of not using the exact spelling on them.

 

Robert Jordan has pulled in Asian names, European names, and more variations 

than most can keep track of.  If it's reasonable 

and you can find it with a google search and it's not a major character in a

popular series/movie, accept it.
