JOBS
Builders are responsible to build and maintain the areas (vnums) assigned

to them by the head builder, and to make sure that they are completed within

a reasonable amount of time (determined by builder and head builder.) 

Builders are also given the reasponsibility of keeping mobiles, equipment

etc.  In line with the overall rules of TSW.  


Coders are responsible for bringing the code up to the level agreed upon by

the coders and head coder and also to fulfill assignments from the head

coder.  Coders are also given the resposibility to fix bugs and provide code

so that the game is in line with the overall goal of TSW.


Other Immortals will work on given assignments, and should know what

their jobs are.

 

If your assignment/job is unclear, see one of the head immortals.
