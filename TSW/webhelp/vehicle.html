vehicle
There are special items known as vehicles here on the ShadowWars.  They

are obtainable when you reach either the Merchant or Noble status, and are

approved for the role.  


To enter a vehicle: enter (vehicle name)


To leave a vehicle: leave


((While in the vehicle))


Drive the vehicle: drive (direction)


Look out of vehicle: look out (vehicle name)


-When you look out of a vehicle, it will only show you the open exits.  You

will not see hidden, or closed exits from inside the vehicle.  


Vehicles can not recall, or be summoned.  They can also not travel through

gates.  So you must remember wher eyou leave them and drive there from one

spot to another.
